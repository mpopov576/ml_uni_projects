The dataset contains information about cities.

Features:
city_name: City identifier
country: Geographic region
population_density: Population per square kilometer
avg_income: Average monthly household income (in USD)
internet_penetration: Percentage of households with internet access
avg_rent: Average monthly apartment rent (in USD)
air_quality_index: Air quality index, where lower values indicate cleaner air
public_transport_score: Quality of public transport, where higher values indicate better performance
happiness_score: Life satisfaction score based on surveys of random citizens, where higher values indicate greater satisfaction
green_space_ratio: Percentage of the city area covered by parks/green spaces
